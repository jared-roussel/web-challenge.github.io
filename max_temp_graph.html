Max Temperature

There is a clear correlation between latitude values and max temperatures. For the scatter plot of latitude vs. max temperature, the plot arches and most values peak for coordinates near 20 degrees N latitude. The plot indicates there is higher correlation for the Northern Hemisphere versus the Southern Hemisphere.
